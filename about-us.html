Sugar, Ice, and all things nice…That’s what cotton candy is made of.

Yamille Mummolo, has always been an entrepreneur of sweetness, and since 2009 she has been doing just that.  Popping-up kiosks and stores across several shopping malls throughout the country, Yamille specializes in the wonderful world of confectionaries and smiles.

Cotton Candy Bar, had spun into its own delicious company when Yamille began slinging the silky sugar inside her Chicago candy store.  “The Sweet & Sticky Sugar Shop” was already a colorful world of confectionary bliss, however, when Yamille wanted to really spin-up the crowd, she would turn on her cotton candy machine to puff clouds of sweet-fluffy goodness.

With a simple twist of fate, sprinkled with some fairy magic,Yamille closed the doors to her Chicago candy shop in 2012, and relocated to the San Francisco Bay Area, along with her family.

Shortly after settling down in her new home, Yamille soon found herself experimenting with her Cotton Candy Machine from her former store.  Her daughter would gaze with excitement watching mommy spin the sweet fluff, as Yamille would stack the rainbow of samples on the kitchen bar…

In January of 2014 “Cotton Candy Bar” puffed-up, and is now a full service, for hire, cotton candy party provider. The goal is to spin the most delicious cotton candy on the planet; for all events, for all ages, anytime, and everywhere.
